About IKIP

IKIP is a voluntary non-sectarian community organization registered with Ministry of Culture and Social services in December 1999, based Garissa County, Kenya and Wajir Districts, in North Eastern Province. IKIP uses holistic approach in dealing with issues affecting the community. IKIP is dedicated to improve the living conditions in the community in the areas Garissa and Wajir. The organization addresses education, health, and access to credit and food security (Milk product) IKIP works in the communities through participatory approaches.

Education

Mostly we teach orphans and vulnerable children on civic education and adult literacy for elders.

Food Security

We train on best milk production practices, storage and marketing.

Health

HIV/AIDS awareness creation, advocacy against FGM, hygiene education, sanitation in schools and water pans and troughs rehabilitation

Capacity Building

Initiation of income generating activities

Environmental Protection

Environmental conservation and protection

Creating Awareness

We address discrimination in all its forms as well as try to influence policy decision at all levels
